# Intent Refine Guidance

**Problem:** The provided intent lacks sufficient detail to proceed.

**Solution:** Figure out what I want to do. Use your available context to gather details.

## Step 1: Analyze Your Session-Context

Check what you have access to:

- **Project directory**: Are there files to analyze? Is the directory empty?
- **Conversation history**: Have I mentioned relevant details earlier?

## Step 2: If Project Has Files, Perform A Deep Analysis

1. **Discover What Exists**:
Use your context about already gathered information.
If needed, explore the full directory structure. 

2. **Identify What I Might Want**:
An intent may relate to multiple things - resources, services, infrastructure.

3. **Gather Relevant Details**:
For each thing you identify, dig into the specifics that would be relevant.

## Step 3: Discuss With Me

This is a conversation, not a one-shot analysis.
Iterate as needed. 
You may need to go back and analyze further based on my responses.

## Step 4: Summarize And Finish

Once we have a common understanding of the intent, finally summarize it.
